Description:

The brigadeiro is a traditional Brazilian dessert that gained hearts all over the world for its texture, amazing flavour and being able to endless combinations with toppings and flavours.

The origin of the dessert isn't certain, but the most common theory is that is was created by a confectioner from Rio de Janeiro, Heloisa Nabuco de Oliveira, to promote the presidential candidacy of Eduardo Gomes.

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Condensed Milk
  • 40g of Cocoa Powder
  • 40g of Butter
  • A handful of your favorite sprinkles and toppings!
